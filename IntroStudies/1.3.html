1.3 Bible Studies: God Loves Us

And We Can Be at Peace with Him

Purpose:

To understand the goodness and power of God so we can trust Him and find hope in Him.

Introduction

One of the most significant religious questions is not whether we love God, but rather, do we believe that He loves us?

If we are convinced of God's love, everything changes: we gain immense dignity, hope for a better life, and assurance of help in our daily struggles. Knowing God loves us allows us to trust Him and love Him in return.

1) Can We Consider God as Both Father and Mother?

📖 Isaiah 49:15 - "Can a woman forget her nursing child and have no compassion on the son of her womb? Even if these may forget, I will not forget you."

God's love is even greater than a mother's, showing His deep and personal care for us.

2) What Defines God's Nature?

📖 1 John 4:8 - "God is love."

This is the most important thing we can say about God—His very essence is love.

3) How Much Does God Love Us?

📖 John 3:16 - "For God so loved the world that He gave His only begotten Son, that whoever believes in Him should not perish but have eternal life."

God’s love is so profound that He sacrificed His own Son for us.

4) Does God Love Us Only When We Are Good?

📖 Romans 5:7-8 - "God demonstrates His own love for us in this: while we were still sinners, Christ died for us."

God's love is unconditional, extending even when we fall short.

5) Who Loved First: God or Us?

📖 1 John 4:19 - "We love because He first loved us."

God took the first step in loving us, inviting us to respond in love.

6) Does God Forgive Us When We Confess Our Sins?

📖 1 John 1:9 - "If we confess our sins, He is faithful and just to forgive us and cleanse us from all unrighteousness."

God's forgiveness is assured when we seek Him in repentance.

7) Does God Care for Each of Us Personally?

📖 Matthew 10:29-30 - "Not even a sparrow falls to the ground without your Father's consent. Even the hairs of your head are all numbered."

Though He is the Lord of the universe, God cares for every detail of our lives.

8) How Did God Show His Love in the Greatest Way?

📖 Matthew 1:23 - "Behold, the virgin shall conceive and bear a Son, and they shall call His name Immanuel, which means 'God with us.'"

Through Jesus, God became one of us so we could approach Him with confidence.

9) What Did Jesus Do to Demonstrate His Love?

📖 Philippians 2:5-8 - "Jesus humbled Himself and became obedient to the point of death, even death on a cross."

Jesus experienced human suffering and ultimately gave His life for us.

10) How Should We Feel Before God?

📖 Hebrews 4:14-16 - "Let us then approach the throne of grace with confidence, so that we may receive mercy and find grace to help us in our time of need."

Knowing who God truly is should bring us peace, not fear.

Key Biblical Texts

📖 Jeremiah 31:3 - God's eternal love.

📖 Isaiah 43:2-3 - God is with us through trials.

📖 Isaiah 49:15 - God's love exceeds even a mother's.

📖 Hosea 11:1 - God cares for His people despite their unfaithfulness.

📖 Matthew 10:29-30 - God values even the smallest things in our lives.

📖 Matthew 11:28-30 - Jesus invites us to find rest in Him.

📖 John 3:16 - God's ultimate sacrifice.

📖 Romans 5:7-8 - God gave His Son even when we were sinners.

📖 Philippians 2:5-8 - Jesus humbled Himself to save us.

📖 Hebrews 4:14-16 - We can confidently approach God.

Conclusion

God's love is beyond human comprehension, but through Jesus, we can know Him personally and live in peace with Him.
