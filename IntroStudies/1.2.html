Lesson 1.2: Does God Exist?

Exploring the Evidence for God's Existence

Purpose:

Understanding the fundamental elements that affirm that God exists and that He is our Creator.

Introduction

Who are we? Are we merely the product of chance? Do we come from nothing and return to nothing, or do we have a Father who created us, who has a plan for us, and who gives us hope for a better future while sustaining us in our daily lives?

The question of God's existence is fundamental to the meaning of life and spirituality. Many believe in God even if they cannot fully explain their faith. Just as love cannot always be explained, faith has its own profound reasons.

1) The Testimony of Nature

Observing the beauty, complexity, and power of nature naturally leads us to recognize a wise and powerful Creator.
