Introduction

Please note that these studies are neither complete nor organized in a strictly systematic mannerâ€”they are designed as resources to help you reflect on your faith and to facilitate discussions with friends. The responses are presented in a conversational style, making them suitable for both individual reading and group conversation.

While the sequence of topics is flexible and can be tailored to individual or group needs, it is important that each study builds on a set of foundational topics, ensuring a deeper understanding of God's love and the generous nature of His teachings.

For those studying in groups, printing and sharing the relevant sections of this collection may enhance the discussion. In future editions, I aim to provide simplified study sheets for each subject area.

Feedback, including error corrections and suggestions, is always welcome. You can reach me at felixcobby@gmail.com.
